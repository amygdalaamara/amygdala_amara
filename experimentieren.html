about amygdala_amara

amygdala_amara is a musician from berlin, who started making
alternative pop music, because studying medicine left too much free
space in her time squedule. It's also rumored, that she doesn't have in
fact too much time, but too much struggle making music and not
failing the exams, but the music thing is much more fun.
